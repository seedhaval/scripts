splunk query
    convert
        convert to number
        convert auto(*)
            convert all fields to numbers
        convert auto(*) none(foo)
            convert all except foo
        convert dur2sec(delay)
            convert duration to seconds
        convert memk(virt)
            convert to KB. all k,m,g converted to k
        convert timeformat='%H:%M:%S' ctime(_time) as c_time
            convert unix time to human readable
    correlate
        calculate correlation between fields
        default limit is 1000 rows
        finds for all and presented as nxn matrix
    erex
        generate regular expression from examples
        erex monthday example="7/01"
        erex monthday example="7/02, 07/02" counterexample="99/2"
        click the job menu to see generated regular expression
